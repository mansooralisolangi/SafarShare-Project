What is Carpool/Ride Sharing?

Carpooling, also known as ride-sharing, is when multiple people travel together in the same vehicle to a common or similar destination. With SafarShare, you can:

Save Money

Split fuel, toll, and maintenance costs with fellow travelers

Reduce Pollution

Fewer vehicles on the road means less carbon footprint

Meet People

Connect with new people traveling the same route

Save Time

Use carpool lanes and reduce traffic congestion

How Carpool Works on SafarShare

1. Create Account

Register as a driver or passenger with verified credentials

2. Post/Find Ride

Drivers post their route and available seats. Passengers search for rides

3. Connect & Confirm

Discuss details, agree on pickup points, and confirm the ride

4. Travel Together

Meet at the agreed location and travel together safely

5. Rate & Review

Share your experience and help build our trusted community

Cost Sharing Example

Karachi to Islamabad

Fuel Cost (One-way) PKR 10,000

Important Notes:

  • SafarShare charges 10% service fee from drivers
  • Payment can be cash or digital (Easypaisa/JazzCash)
  • Women-only rides available for safety
  • Insurance coverage for all verified rides
