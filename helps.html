Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Account & Registration

Yes! Creating an account on SafarShare is completely free. You only pay for the rides you book or the services you use.

You need a valid phone number, email address, and some basic personal information. For drivers, we require additional verification including driver's license and vehicle documents.

Absolutely! One account can be used for both riding and driving. You can switch between modes based on your needs.

Booking & Rides

Enter your pickup and drop-off locations in the search bar. SafarShare will show you all available rides matching your route along with prices and driver ratings.

Yes, you can book rides up to 7 days in advance. This is especially useful for planned trips and daily commutes.

You can cancel your booking through the app or website. Cancellation policies vary based on timing - check the specific ride details for cancellation terms.

Payments & Pricing

Fares are calculated based on distance, time, demand, and vehicle type. You'll see the exact fare before confirming your booking.

We accept cash payments, mobile wallets (JazzCash, EasyPaisa), and credit/debit cards for online payments.

SafarShare charges a small service fee (10-15%) from drivers for each completed ride. This helps us maintain and improve our platform.

Safety & Security

We verify all users, provide emergency contact features, allow ride tracking, and have a 24/7 support team. All drivers undergo background checks.

Use the emergency button in the app to alert authorities and SafarShare support. Always share your ride details with trusted contacts.

Drivers must submit valid driver's license, vehicle registration, insurance, and pass a background check before they can offer rides.

Your Safety Guide

Important safety tips for riders and drivers

Verify Before You Travel

  • Check driver ratings and reviews
  • Verify vehicle details match the app
  • Confirm the driver knows your name

Share Your Trip

  • Share ride details with family/friends
  • Use the in-app sharing feature
  • Keep someone updated on your location

Stay Connected

  • Keep your phone charged
  • Save emergency contacts
  • Use in-app messaging for communication

Trust Your Instincts

  • Cancel if something feels wrong
  • Report suspicious behavior
  • Choose well-lit pickup/drop points
